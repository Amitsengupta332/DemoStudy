About GlobalPath Australia

Guiding Students Toward Australian Opportunities

GlobalPath Australia is an education consultancy dedicated to helping international students access world-class education opportunities in Australia. We provide personalised, outcome-focused guidance for students who want to study, grow, and succeed.

Founded by an ex-international student from Bangladesh with deep understanding of both Australia and Bangladesh, and in partnership with a highly reputed Bangladeshi industrialist, we combine local expertise with a global perspective.
